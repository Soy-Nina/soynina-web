Comprehensive sex education is much more than talking about sexuality. It is a fundamental right that prepares girls and adolescents to make informed decisions about their bodies, relationships, and futures.

## What Is Comprehensive Sex Education?

Comprehensive sex education (CSE) is a teaching-learning approach that covers five key dimensions:

1. **Biological:** Knowledge about the body, pubertal development, and reproduction
2. **Psychological:** Emotions, gender identity, and sexual orientation
3. **Social:** Relationships, communication, and interpersonal skills
4. **Ethical:** Values, human rights, and responsibility
5. **Preventive:** Information about sexually transmitted infections, early pregnancy, and gender-based violence

## Proven Benefits

Scientific research demonstrates that comprehensive sex education:

- Reduces teenage pregnancy
- Decreases sexually transmitted infections
- Improves communication in relationships
- Strengthens the ability to identify and prevent sexual violence
- Increases self-esteem and responsible decision-making
