At Soy Niña, every day we witness transformations that remind us why we do this work. The stories of our participants are stories of hope, resilience, and positive change.

## Sofía's Story

Sofía came to our Club Niña program two years ago, shy and with low self-esteem. In her own words, "I didn't know I could be whatever I wanted to be." Through our empowerment workshops, recreational activities, and ongoing support, Sofía not only gained confidence in herself but became a leader within the group.

Today, Sofía is one of our change advocates. She helps guide new participants and shares her story to inspire other girls to believe in their potential.

## Measurable Impact

The data supports our stories:

- 85% improvement in self-esteem among our participants
- 500+ girls and adolescents directly impacted
- 150+ families strengthened through our programs
- 200+ volunteers committed to the mission

## Beyond the Numbers

But numbers don't tell the whole story. What really matters is seeing the sparkle in a girl's eyes when she realizes she has the power to change her life. It's hearing a mother say that her daughter now speaks with confidence at home. It's witnessing how an adolescent who was a victim of abuse empowers herself through education and community support.

These are the stories that inspire us to continue, to grow, and to reach more communities in Costa Rica.
